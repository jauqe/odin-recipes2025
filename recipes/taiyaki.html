equipment:

  • taiyaki pan

ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cup cake flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar use 3 if you prefer it to be sweeter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • olive oil or any neutral oil

instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, sift together cake flour, baking soda, baking powder. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, crack and whisk an egg.
  3. Add sugar and combine with the egg.
  4. Pour in the milk and water and mix well.
  5. Add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix well.
  6. Once combined, cover and place the batter in the fridge for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  7. When the batter is ready to use, preheat a taiyaki pan over low heat and brush with oil.
  8. Turn the heat off and pour batter into the molds until about 60% full. Add filling and then pour the batter over the filling until the molds are full.
  9. Turn the heat onto medium-low and cook on one side for 2 to 2 1/2 minutes. Flip the pan and cook about the same amount on the other side, or until both sides are golden.
  10. Remove from the pan and enjoy immediately. いただきます!
